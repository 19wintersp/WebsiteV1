About

Hi! I'm a human who tells computers what to do.

My first "proper" programming language was Python, however I mostly use NodeJS, Web Technologies and C++ now, with an interest in Rust. I'll also occasionally use Java and C#, though the the JVM and .NET Framework annoy me a little. I also want to use Deno, but Replit is being a bit annoying at the moment by using a version that's horribly out of date.

At the moment, I'm working on a few projects, which you can see more of under Projects. My main foci are a video editor, a pair of MIDI-related applications in Rust, and a fully-featured voice- and text- chat application.
